What is Digitalising Kashmir?

Digitalising Kashmir is a joint initiative by Four Seasons and Hackesta to help the artists and photographers of Kashmir, and make an online Portfolio for them, at the lowest cost.

Portfolio Features

  • User Friendly

    You won't need to upload your art/photographs to the website.
    It will automatically fetch them from 500px or Deviant Art

  • Secure Website

    All portfolios come with free HTTPS

  • Low Cost Website

    Websites starting from as low as 2300 INR
