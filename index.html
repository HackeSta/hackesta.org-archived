Hack-e-Sta

Before falling in love with coding, I was very interested in hacking. I got my first computer when I was 12, and was hacking using the Reset Password trick. I spent three years with hacking related stuff. I started many blogs with different friends of mine. I gave up on all of them after a while.

Then Hack-e-Sta happened. I was in the final stages of my 8th Class, and attending my computer practicals in the school computer lab. I had planned on starting another blog with a friend, and we were thinking for a cool name. Hack-e-Sta came to mind, and I started it as a blog to teach different computer tricks. I kept that up for a few months, and then in the beginnng of my 9th Class, I stumbled upon Visual Studio, and I fell in love with coding. My initial projects were all in C#, for a gaming server I used to play on.
