KPC

Beautiful Photographs

At your finger tips


Beauty of Kashmir is just a tap away

KPC Android App lets you view photos taken by members of Kashmir Photography Club, showing you the best of Kashmir.

Features

Multiple Sections

All Photos:
View photos without any filter or sorting

Categories:
View photos by choosing a category (Landscapes, Street Photography, etc)

Favorites
View photos which ahve been favorited by you

Offline Mode

You can use the app offline as well. The app stores the recent photos viewed by you, so incase you don't have an internet connection, you can still view previously viewed photos

Favorites

You can favorite any photo and view them in a seperate section. How?

While viewing the photo in full screen view, press the heart button to favorite it. You can then go to the favorites section in the main screen to view the photos.

Predefined Caption

You can set a predefined caption (e.g, your social account links, your website, etc), which can later be added while posting a new photo. How?

Go to the app settings and set your caption. The next time you are posting, click on the 'Use Defined Caption' switch to add the caption.

Share the photo

You can share the viewed photo with people, and can also give credit to the photographer & KPC How?

While viewing the photo in full screen view, press the share button to share the photo.

Download the photo

You can download a high quality version of any photo. How?

While viewing the photo in full screen view, press the download button to start the download. The photos will be downloaded to Pictures/KPC

Multiple Qualities

You can set the quality you want to use while browsing in the app settings, and also choose the resolution of the photo you are downloading.


FAQ

Is it necessary to login with Facebook?

No it isn't. Using a Facebook gives access to features like commenting on the photo, and uploading you own photo.

How can I logout/log in again?

The logout/login button is present in the app settings.

The thumbnails aren't loading/are appearing white. Is that supposed to happen?

That is normal, and happens to reduce load on your internet.

Why isn't the Like Button working?

Facebook doesn't allow users logged into third party apps like KPC which use Graph API to publish likes.

It disabled this feature on November 17,2016 according to this documentation.
