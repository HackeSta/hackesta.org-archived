Kashmir Photography Club

Kashmir Photography Club, popularly known as KPC is a group of photography enthusiasts, mostly from Kashmir but includes members from outside Kashmir as well. Started way back in 2004 with a handful of members, the group has progressed to be the largest photography group in the region. Other than its regular members on ground, the group is spread across Facebook, Twitter and Instagram with an accumulated strength of more than 1 lac members and growing!

Other than holding regular photography related events such as photo-walks, photo exhibitions, workshops etc., KPC continues to serve as a learning and grooming platform for young and budding photographers.
