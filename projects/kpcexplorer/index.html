At your finger tips


Beauty of Kashmir is just a tap away

KPCExplorer lets you view photos taken by members of Kashmir Photography Club, showing you the best of Kashmir.

Features

Multiple Sections

All Photos:
View photos without any filter or sorting

Categories:
View photos by choosing a category (Landscapes, Street Photography, etc)

Favorites
View photos which ahve been favorited by you

Offline Mode

You can use the app offline as well. The app stores the recent photos viewed by you, so incase you don't have an internet connection, you can still view previously viewed photos

Favorites

You can favorite any photo and view them in a seperate section

Share the photo

You can share the viewed photo with people, and can also give credit to the photographer & KPC

Download the photo

You can download a high quality version of any photo


FAQ

Is it necessary to login with Facebook?

No it isn't. Using a Facebook gives access to features like commenting on the photo, and uploading you own photo.

How can I logout/log in again?

The logout/login button is present in the app settings.

The thumbnails aren't loading/are appearing white. Is that supposed to happen?

That is normal, and happens to reduce load on your internet.

Why isn't the Like Button working?

Facebook doesn't allow users logged into third party apps like KPCExplorer which use Graph API to publish likes.

It disabled this feature on November 17,2016 according to this documentation.
